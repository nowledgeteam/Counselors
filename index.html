How It Works

Every milestone. Four touchpoints. Nothing slips through.

College planning has more moving parts than any family can hold in their head. For each of the 25 most critical milestones, Nowledge sends four carefully timed messages — so families are always informed, never caught off guard.

💰 FAFSA Filing 12th Grade
60 Days FAFSA opens October 1 — gather tax returns and financial documents now so you can file immediately.
30 Days One month until FAFSA opens. Many schools award aid on a first-come basis — filing early can make a real difference.
7 Days FAFSA opens in one week. Confirm your FSA ID is active and all documents are ready to go.
Last Call FAFSA opens tomorrow. File as soon as it opens — do not wait.
📝 Junior PSAT / National Merit 11th Grade
60 Days The junior-year PSAT is the one that qualifies students for National Merit recognition. It's 60 days away — time to prepare.
30 Days PSAT is next month. If your student is aiming for National Merit, now is the time for focused practice on weak areas.
7 Days PSAT is next week. Confirm the testing date at your student's school and make sure they know the location and start time.
Last Call PSAT is this week. Good rest and a solid breakfast matter more than last-minute cramming.
